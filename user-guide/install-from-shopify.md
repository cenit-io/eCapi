**App installation process**

# Find OMNA at Shopify store

Go to your administration Link of Shopify Store, and find out OMNA in the search box. 

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/installation_0.jpg"/>
</div>

Shopify will redirect you to https://apps.shopify.com/omna-2?surface_detail=OMNA&surface_inter_position=1&surface_intra_position=3&surface_type=search

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/installation_1.jpg"/>
</div>

Click on Add App to start the installation process, and OMNA will open you a new tab with the installation process:

## Login
OMNA will show you a login page where you should write your user and password for use OMNA app.

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/installation_01.jpg"/>
</div>


After your enroll with user and password. OMNA will be installed in your Shopify Administration:

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/installation_02.jpg"/>
</div>

Please, click on "Install App"

## First OMNA configuration

After click Install App, OMNA will show you this firts page:

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/installation_2.jpg"/>
</div>

This part is indicating you that OMNA is configuring the initials settings to could run the app in your store.
It implies:
- configuration of the new user for use OMNA app.
- in your space, it is installing OMNA and Shopify collections with all its functionalities.
- principal flows configurations: Import orders.

This process could spent as normal one or two minutes of your time. After that, OMNA will open its homepage:

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/home.jpg"/>
</div>

At this point OMNA is installed successfully in your Shopify store. If you go back to your Shopify administration, you could see OMNA installed.

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/omna_installed.jpg"/>
</div>

If you open this new link, you will see an embebed application.

<div align=center>
    <img width="800" src="/assets/images/installation/dashboard.jpg"/>
</div>

This view will have a link to your OMNA workspace (Go to OMNA).

>To know how start to use OMNA app, please consult a [Quick Start](quick-start)
